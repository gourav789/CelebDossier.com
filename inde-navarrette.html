Introduction

Inde Navarrette is a young American actress who has quickly become one of the most talked-about new faces in Hollywood.

Many fans first met her as Sarah Cushing in the superhero drama Superman & Lois. That role ran for four seasons and turned her into a familiar name for television viewers.

She then made a big leap to film. Her chilling lead performance in the horror movie Obsession earned strong reviews and pushed her into the spotlight as a real breakout star.

With a mix of heart, grit, and range, Inde has shown she can shine on both the small screen and the big screen.

Early Life

Inde was born Danielle Fabiola Navarrette on March 3, 2001, in Tucson, Arizona. She grew up in a warm, multicultural home.

Her father is of Mexican heritage and served in the United States Marine Corps. Her mother, Tossi Fox, is originally from Australia and works as a hairstylist.

Because of her father's military service, the family moved often during her childhood. Instead of feeling unsettled, Inde treated each new city as a chance to start fresh and grow.

During one of these moves she picked up the nickname "Inde," a nod to her strong, independent spirit. The name stuck, and she now uses it professionally.

She has spoken warmly about her Mexican roots, which added personal meaning to some of her later acting work.

Career

Career Beginnings

Inde started out with small screen and short film work in her late teens. She appeared in the short films Cross Words Together and Cranberry Nights, which helped her build early experience.

In 2019 she landed one of her first regular roles as Veronica in the Snapchat comedy series Denton's Death Date.

Breakthrough on Television

A major step came in 2020 when she joined the fourth and final season of the drama 13 Reasons Why. She played Estela de la Cruz, a role that put her in front of a huge streaming audience.

That same year she appeared in the emotional drama film Wander Darkly, adding a feature credit to her growing resume.

Major Projects

Her biggest television role arrived in 2021 with Superman & Lois, where she starred as Sarah Cushing. The series ran through 2024 and gave her the chance to grow a character across four seasons.

On the show she shared many scenes with a large ensemble cast, including Tyler Hoechlin and Elizabeth Tulloch in the lead roles.

Recent Work

In 2025 Inde took on a supporting part in the action thriller Trap House. She then delivered the performance that changed everything.

She starred as Nikki Freeman in the horror film Obsession, which premiered at a major film festival before reaching wide audiences. Critics praised both her tender and terrifying sides, and awards buzz soon followed.
