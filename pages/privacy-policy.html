Introduction

This Privacy Policy applies to all visitors and users of CelebDossier (celebdossier.com). It describes how we collect, use, store, and protect information when you access or interact with our website.

By accessing or using CelebDossier, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with any part of this policy, we encourage you to discontinue use of the website.

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or applicable regulations. We encourage you to review this page periodically for the latest information.

Information We Collect

Personal Information

We do not require users to create accounts or provide personal information to browse CelebDossier. However, you may voluntarily provide certain information when contacting us, such as:

  • Name
  • Email Address
  • Contact Information

This information is only collected when you choose to submit it through our contact forms or email communication.

Automatically Collected Information

When you visit CelebDossier, certain information may be collected automatically through standard web technologies. This may include:

  • IP Address
  • Browser Type
  • Device Information
  • Operating System
  • Referring Pages
  • Visited Pages
  • Date & Time of Visit
  • Session Information
  • General Analytics Data

This data is used in aggregate form to understand how visitors interact with the website and to improve overall performance.

How We Use Information

Purpose of Data Usage

The information collected may be used for the following purposes:

  • Improve website performance and reliability
  • Enhance and personalize user experience
  • Respond to user inquiries and feedback
  • Maintain website security and prevent abuse
  • Analyze visitor behavior and traffic patterns
  • Detect spam and malicious activity
  • Develop new features and content
  • Improve overall content quality and organization

We do not sell, rent, or trade personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

Cookies

How We Use Cookies

CelebDossier may use cookies — small text files stored on your device — to enhance your experience. Cookies help us to:

  • Remember user preferences and settings
  • Improve website functionality and navigation
  • Measure website performance and load times
  • Understand visitor behavior and usage patterns
  • Provide more personalized browsing experiences

You can control or disable cookies through your browser settings at any time. Please note that disabling cookies may affect certain features or functionality of the website.

Advertising

Google AdSense

CelebDossier may display advertisements served through Google AdSense, a third-party advertising service provided by Google LLC.

Google may use cookies to serve advertisements based on your previous visits to this website or other websites on the internet. Third-party vendors, including Google, may use cookies to serve personalized or non-personalized advertisements depending on your preferences and applicable regulations.

You can manage your advertising preferences and opt out of personalized advertising through Google's Ad Settings at any time. You may also visit the Network Advertising Initiative opt-out page to manage third-party advertising cookies.

For more information about how Google uses data when you use partner sites, please refer to Google's Privacy & Terms page.

Analytics

Google Analytics

CelebDossier may use Google Analytics, a web analytics service provided by Google LLC, to help understand how visitors interact with the website. Google Analytics uses cookies and collects aggregated data including:

  • Number of visitors and sessions
  • Traffic sources and referral information
  • Most popular pages and content
  • User engagement metrics
  • Device and browser usage statistics

All analytics information is collected in aggregate form and is used solely to improve the website's content, structure, and overall user experience. No personally identifiable information is associated with analytics data.

Third-Party Services

Services We May Use

CelebDossier may use trusted third-party services to support the operation, performance, and functionality of the website. These services may include, where applicable:

  • Google Analytics — for website analytics and visitor insights
  • Google AdSense — for advertising and monetization
  • Cloudflare — for security, performance, and content delivery
  • Content Delivery Networks (CDNs) — for faster asset loading
  • Social Media Platforms — for content sharing and embeds

Each of these third-party services operates under its own privacy policy and terms of service. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any third-party services you interact with through our website.

Third-Party Links

CelebDossier may contain links to external websites, services, or resources that are not operated or controlled by us. These links are provided for convenience and informational purposes only.

We are not responsible for the content, privacy practices, or policies of any third-party websites. We encourage you to review the privacy policy of every website you visit. Clicking on a third-party link or visiting a third-party website is done entirely at your own discretion and risk.

Data Security

Protecting Your Information

We take the security of your information seriously. CelebDossier employs reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect user information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

However, please be aware that no method of internet transmission or electronic storage is guaranteed to be 100% secure. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your information, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

Children's Privacy

CelebDossier is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect, solicit, or store personal information from anyone under the age of 13.

If we become aware that we have inadvertently collected personal information from a child under 13, we will take steps to remove that information from our records as soon as reasonably possible. If you believe that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us through our Contact page so we can take appropriate action.

Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have certain rights regarding your personal information. You may request:

Request access to the personal information we hold about you.

Correction

Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete information.

Deletion

Request deletion of your personal information from our records.

Consent Withdrawal

Withdraw consent where applicable under your local laws.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us through our Contact page. We will respond to your request within a reasonable timeframe.

Changes to this Privacy Policy become effective immediately upon publication unless otherwise stated. Your continued use of CelebDossier after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically to stay informed about how we protect your information.
